Opportune Hub

A unified platform that connects students with internships, hackathons, certifications, placements, and career opportunities — all in one intelligent hub.

Students today waste hours scattered across dozens of portals — one site for internships, another for hackathons, another for certifications. Opportune Hub eliminates that fragmentation by aggregating every career-building opportunity into a single, personalised dashboard.

The platform intelligently surfaces relevant opportunities based on a student's branch, year, and skill profile — turning passive browsing into active career development.

  • Aggregated opportunity feed

    Internships, hackathons, certifications, placements, and events pulled into one scrollable feed with filter and search.

  • Personalised student profile

    Students build a profile with skills, branch, and year — used to rank and recommend the most relevant opportunities first.

  • Deadline tracker & reminders

    Visual countdown timers and optional email alerts so students never miss an application deadline again.

  • Admin portal for colleges

    Placement coordinators can post drives, track applications, and export placement reports directly from the platform.

  • Resource library

    Curated prep materials — aptitude guides, resume templates, interview question banks — linked directly to active listings.

01
Data aggregation at scale

Pulling live listings from heterogeneous sources (LinkedIn, Internshala, Devfolio) without a unified API required building a robust scraping and normalisation layer with rate-limit handling.

02
Relevance ranking without ML

Matching students to opportunities meaningfully before introducing ML required a hand-crafted scoring algorithm weighing skills overlap, deadline proximity, and application volume.

03
Role-based access complexity

Supporting three distinct roles — student, admin, and company — with separate dashboards and permissions within a single codebase required careful JWT scoping and middleware architecture.

04
Mobile-first performance

Most students browse on low-end phones on college Wi-Fi. Achieving sub-2s load time required aggressive lazy loading, image optimisation, and CDN-backed static assets.

AI-powered matching

Integrating an NLP-based recommendation engine that learns from application history to progressively improve opportunity relevance.

Resume builder

An in-platform resume editor that auto-fills from the student profile and exports ATS-optimised PDFs tailored to each application.

Mock interview module

AI-driven mock interviews with role-specific question banks and feedback on answer quality, confidence indicators, and keyword coverage.

College partnerships

White-label deployment for placement cells at partner institutions, with custom branding and college-specific opportunity pipelines.

Mobile app

A React Native companion app with push notifications for deadline alerts and new opportunity drops — keeping students informed without opening a browser.

Analytics dashboard

Placement insights for college administrators: application funnel rates, company return rates, and offer trend analysis across departments and years.
